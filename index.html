Why screenshots?

Universo da Doçura is a custom WordPress build still in active development. Hosting a screenshot gallery lets me share the design system, layout decisions, and content structure without exposing an unfinished backend or paying for hosting before it’s ready.

It’s basically a “museum exhibit” of the site.
